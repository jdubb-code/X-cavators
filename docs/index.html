About the Game

X-cavators is an iOS game where you pilot a rover to excavate artifacts across a scrolling world. Manage your battery, collect as many artifacts as you can, and see how far you can go.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • The app crashed or won't open. Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall from the App Store.
  • My progress was lost. Progress is stored locally on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app will reset your game.
  • The game is not responding to taps. Make sure your screen is clean and try restarting the app.
