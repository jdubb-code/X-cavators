Privacy Policy — Last updated March 5, 2026

Overview

X-cavators is a single-player game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

Data We Do Not Collect

X-cavators does not collect:

— Personal information (name, email, address)
— Device identifiers or advertising IDs
— Location data
— Usage analytics or crash reports sent to us
— Any information from your contacts, camera, or microphone

Local Storage

Game progress (such as scores) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

X-cavators does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no personal data from anyone, X-cavators is safe for users of all ages. We comply with COPPA and similar regulations.
